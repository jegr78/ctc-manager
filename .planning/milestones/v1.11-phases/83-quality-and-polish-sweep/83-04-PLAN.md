<objective>
Implement QUAL-04: Replace `resolvedPhase.getGroups()` lazy access at `StandingsController.java:138` with an explicit service-layer call returning a fully-resolved view object. Controller becomes plumbing-only; OSIV-lazy traversal is eliminated from controller code.

Purpose: CLAUDE.md "Keep Controllers Thin" requires no business logic in controllers, and "OSIV" guidance prefers `@EntityGraph` / service-layer materialization over lazy traversal during template render. This plan introduces a `StandingsView` record DTO and a `StandingsViewService` that owns the entire resolution flow (alltime, explicit phase, legacy seasonId, active-season fallback, no-params bare-page). Controller reduces from ~120 lines of resolution logic to ~25 lines of plumbing. Per D-23 the planner picks **Option (a) flat-model-unfurl** so existing `StandingsControllerTest` assertions stay green without template rewrites.

Output: New record + new service in one feature commit (with unit tests), controller refactor + test-update in same commit (D-25: same-commit preferred).

Fix the Provisional-Scores graphic so it pads both the home and away row lists to
a fixed 6 rows. Today buildContext adds only the rows that have real results, so a
team with fewer than 6 results renders fewer rows (the visible SC3 inconsistency).

mirroring the Lineup/Results behavior established in plan 108-01.

Purpose: Closes LINEUP-03 (Provisional-Scores padded to 6 with "n/a").

updated unit-test assertions for the 6-row pad.

Decisions addressed (108-CONTEXT.md): D-01 (render-time padding in the graphic
service, no persisted rows), D-03 (padding driven by the real-result-row count),
D-04 (a roster driver with no result yet shows as "n/a", the mid-import
provisional case), D-06 (reads the central `TEAM_DRIVERS` constant, no second
literal 6).

buildContext (~L60-117) currently:
  - builds homeRows / awayRows by iterating race.getResults() and routing each via
    scoringService.isDriverInTeam(result, raceId, homeTeamId)
  - computes overall point sums from those rows
  - sets ctx variables homeRows / awayRows (and the overall sums)
  NO padding exists today → fewer than 6 results = fewer than 6 rows.

provisional-scores-render.html iterates `th:each="row : ${homeRows}"` / `${awayRows}`
and prints row.driverName + numeric columns directly (no empty-string guard today).
A padded row with driverName "n/a" and 0s renders as a plain "n/a" row.

Pad target: AbstractGraphicService.TEAM_DRIVERS (= 6), introduced in plan 108-01.
toRow(result) builds a real ProvisionalRow; add a sibling that returns an "n/a"/0 row.

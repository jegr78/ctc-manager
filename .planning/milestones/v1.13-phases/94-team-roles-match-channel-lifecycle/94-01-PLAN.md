## Objective

Deliver CHAN-01 in three coherent slices on the active branch `gsd/v1.13-discord-integration`:
1. The Flyway V9 bundled migration adds `teams.discord_role_id` (nullable) and `discord_global_config.current_match_category_id` (NOT NULL DEFAULT '').
2. The shared `DiscordSnowflake.PATTERN` constant replaces 5 inline `@Pattern` regexes in `DiscordConfigForm` and is adopted by the new `TeamForm.discordRoleId` field (D-14). `TeamManagementService.save()` extends its positional signature with `discordRoleId`. `DiscordConfigForm` gains a 7th field `currentMatchCategoryId` (D-02).
3. A new `DiscordRoleCache` clone of `DiscordEmojiCache` lands; `DiscordConfigController.refreshRolesCache()` is extended to call `discordRoleCache.refresh(roles)` after `fetchGuildRoles()`. `team-form.html` renders a `.searchable-dropdown` when the cache is warm, plain-text + `.badge-warning` fallback otherwise. `admin.css` ships the `.discord-actions` responsive-wrap cluster (D-06) that closes the UAT-03 mobile overflow on `/admin/discord-config` and is reused by 94-02 + 94-03.

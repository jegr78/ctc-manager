<objective>
Wire the POST-02 Team Cards hybrid trigger (auto-post-on-channel-create per D-95-01 + permanent Re-Post / Refresh buttons per D-95-02) on top of the Plan 95-01 skeleton. `DiscordChannelService.createMatchChannel` gains an end-of-method hook to `discordPostService.postTeamCards(match)`; failure leaves the channel + webhook persisted (D-95-01a + RESEARCH Landmine 6). The Match-Detail Discord-Actions-Panel placeholder (line 56-58, prepared in Phase 94) receives 3 buttons: Post Team Cards / Re-Post Team Cards / ↻ Refresh Cards.

`postTeamCards(Match)` does auto-gen-on-demand via `TeamCardService.cardExists()` → `generateCard()` (synchronous Playwright headless ~10-30s/card, worst case ~60s per RESEARCH Landmine 8). The Refresh endpoint always regenerates both cards and re-posts.

**API-compatibility constraint (binding):** `DiscordChannelService.createMatchChannel(Match)` MUST keep its current `public void` signature. Phase-94 callers (`MatchController.createDiscordChannel` + 4 ITs: `DiscordChannelServiceWireMockIT`, `DiscordChannelServicePermissionAuditFailIT`, `DiscordChannelServiceCleanupFailIT`, `DiscordChannelArchiveServiceWireMockIT`) consume the `void` shape and changing it would break compilation mid-execution. The auto-post error category is propagated to the controller via a request-scoped attribute (`RequestContextHolder.currentRequestAttributes().setAttribute("discord.autoPostError", category, SCOPE_REQUEST)`) set inside the hook's catch block. No new DTO record (`ChannelCreationResult`) is introduced.

Per RESEARCH Landmine 6 the auto-post hook uses **exception swallowing** (catch + log + WARN + record category in the request-scoped attribute; the channel + webhook persist). This is simpler than `REQUIRES_NEW` transaction propagation and matches D-95-01a literally.
</objective>

<objective>
Unify the three divergent team-attribution paths in `DriverRankingService` (`calculateRankingForPhase`, `aggregateAcrossPhases`, the private `calculateAlltimeRanking`) under ONE shared helper implementing the locked policy: **home-first** (D-01 — a rostered driver is attributed to their `SeasonDriver` team), **fallback fielding** (D-02 — a pure guest is attributed to the `RaceLineup` team with sub-team→parent rollup, never null), applied **uniformly** across all surfaces (D-03). Close the `calculateAlltimeRanking` `team = null` gap for pure guests (D-04).

This is the core refactor of Phase 114 and satisfies SCORE-02 (personal crediting with correct team attribution, including drivers without a `SeasonDriver`). Per CLAUDE.md "Grep All Usages Before Refactor", this plan includes an explicit audit-all-callers task and updates the existing unit tests whose mocks are affected by the new `SeasonDriver` lookup (Mockito STRICT_STUBS risk — documented below).

Purpose: Today three paths disagree (per-phase uses lineup-only without parent rollup; aggregate uses REGULAR-team-preference; alltime uses SeasonDriver-only → null for pure guests). A guest's personal ranking row must be correct and consistent everywhere.
Output: Unified `resolveAttributedTeam`; the two legacy private helpers removed/replaced; alltime fallback; full unit coverage.
</objective>

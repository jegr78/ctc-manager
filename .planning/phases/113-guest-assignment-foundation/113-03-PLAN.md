<objective>
Wire the admin lineup form end-to-end for guests: the controller parses `guest_*` params and feeds the GET model (`allDrivers`, `guestLineups`), and `race-lineup.html` gains a per-team Add-Guest block using a native `<datalist>` typeahead, sub-team select, and prefill of existing guests.
